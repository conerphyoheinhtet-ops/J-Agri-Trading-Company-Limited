OUR STORY

Rooted in Tradition, Engineered for Scale

J Agri Trading stands as the strategic link between the fertile farmlands of Myanmar—spanning from the sun-drenched paddies of the Ayeyarwady Delta to the productive agricultural hubs of Bago, Naypyidaw, and Oaktwin—and the global dinner table. Our foundation is built on decades of deep-rooted relationships with local farming communities across these diverse regions, ensuring that every grain we trade carries the integrity of its origin.

By combining ancestral agricultural wisdom with sophisticated industrial scaling, we have transformed from a regional merchant into a premier international exporter. We don’t just move commodities; we manage a legacy of excellence that begins in the rich soil of Myanmar’s heartland and extends to partners worldwide.

TECHNOLOGICAL EXCELLENCE

Precision Processing & Rigorous Standards

At J Agri, we combine advanced engineering with strict standards. Our automated 80-ton daily line utilizes precision mechanics and AI optical sorting for export-grade purity.

High-Frequency Gravity Destoning

Uses micro-vibrations and vacuum airflow to isolate and eliminate dense impurities, ensuring 100% stone-free grain.

Precision Friction Whitening

Advanced LAMICO friction whiteners cleanly remove the outer bran layer, maximizing head-rice yield while preventing breakage.

Optical Color Sorting

High-speed optical sorters scan thousands of grains per second via HD cameras, instantly purging color defects.
